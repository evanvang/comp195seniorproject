About Me

My name is Ayden and I am a third year Computer Science major attending University of the Pacific in Stockton, CA. After attending Saint Marys High School, I chose to attend Las Positas Community College at the recommendation of one of my professors at Saint Marys. With no idea of what I wanted to pursue, a good friend put me on the world of technology. From there I chose to pursue a Computer Science degree while earning certifications through CompTIA. The pandemic paired with the desire to stay with family led me to choose Pacific. Since highschool, I have been able to earn three certifications, become familiar with around five programming languages, and obtain a part time job in the Information technology department in the University of the Pacifics Eberhardt School of Buisness.
